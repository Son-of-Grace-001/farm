WHY CHOOSE FARMERS AID ?

Famers Aid is a platform, that provides information about crops and animals, such as when a crop can be planted, the suitable soil and weather condition, the pests and diseases that affect the crop, the pests and diseases control, the suitable fertilizers and the types of that particular crops and which type is best suitable for each region and also gives adequate information about poultry birds.
